# Bundle — Brand Brief

## Positioning

**Bundle** is a baby names app for anyone naming a new arrival — couples, single parents, grandparents, extended family. The collaboration is a feature, not the identity. The baby is the centre.

## One-liner

**Bundle. Find your bundle's name.**

## Alternates

- Bundle. Baby names, swiped together.
- Bundle. The right name for your new arrival.
- Bundle. Names for the one you love most.

## What it does (App Store description seed)

> Swipe through thousands of baby names together. When everyone in your bundle agrees on a name, it's a match. Add notes, run a final-three tournament, and get AI suggestions that learn from the names you love.
>
> Built for couples, single parents, and families. No subscriptions. One small unlock for the power tools.

## Voice

- Warm, calm, lower-case-leaning where it feels right
- Never twee or babyfied — "bundle" is the only soft word that earns its place
- British English by default; American spellings on the US App Store listing

## Typography

- **Headings:** *Fraunces* (already in the codebase) — serif, soft, distinctive
- **Body:** *Geist Sans* (already in the codebase) — clean, modern
- **App Store screenshots:** Fraunces SemiBold for the name, Geist for everything else

## Icon concepts

Three SVGs alongside this brief:
- `icon-1-swaddle.svg` — abstract swaddled bundle with a heart cutout. Reads as a baby silhouette at small sizes.
- `icon-2-gift.svg` — bundle tied with a bow, minimalist. Reads as a gift / new arrival.
- `icon-3-monogram.svg` — a stylized **b** in Fraunces with a small dot for a face / period. Wordmark variant.

## App Store listing seed

- **Name:** Bundle
- **Subtitle:** Baby names, swipe to match
- **Category:** Lifestyle (primary), Family (secondary)
- **Keywords (100 chars):** `baby names,baby,name,nursery,parenting,couples,swipe,match,family,maternity,pregnant,baby boy,baby girl`
- **Age:** 4+ (no in-app browser, no UGC visible to others)
- **Privacy:** "Data Not Collected" once you swap the headers for proper Sign in with Apple — you literally don't need any PII for swipes

## Pricing

- **Free:** unlimited swiping, unlimited matches, meaning + origin, basic AI generation, push notifications
- **Bundle Pro — £3.99 one-time:** tournament, AI similar-to-your-likes, phonetic variants, notes, custom emoji, all future Pro features

One-time. No subscription. Directly undercuts NameHatch (£7/mo) and BabyName's nickel-and-dime IAPs.

## Marketing surfaces (cheap, high-leverage)

1. Instagram Reels around "names with hidden meanings" + "names that aged terribly" — clip-friendly
2. TikTok partnering with parenting micro-influencers (£0–£200 per post for nano accounts)
3. Reddit /r/namenerds — engage genuinely, never spam
4. ASO: solid screenshots beat ad spend at this scale
5. Press: One Birmingham Live / Manchester Evening News story per major UK city on "two parents from X used an AI app to name their baby" — local press eats these
